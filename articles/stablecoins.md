# Stablecoins

Stablecoins are cryptocurrencies designed to stay equal to $1.

## Why Stablecoins Exist
- Easy global payments  
- Faster and cheaper than banks  
- Useful for trading crypto  
- Avoiding market volatility  

## Popular Stablecoins
- **USDT** (Tether)  
- **USDC** (Circle)  
- **DAI** (Decentralized stablecoin)

## How They Stay Stable
- Backed by real assets  
- Algorithmic stabilization (DAI)  

Stablecoins make crypto practical for everyday use.
