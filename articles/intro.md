# Introduction to Crypto

Cryptocurrency is digital money that works without banks or governments.  
It runs on **blockchain**, a public and transparent network that anyone can verify.

## Why Crypto Matters
- Borderless payments  
- Full control over your money  
- Lower fees  
- Transparent and secure  

## Popular Cryptocurrencies
- Bitcoin (BTC) – digital gold  
- Ethereum (ETH) – smart contracts  
- Solana (SOL) – fast transactions  
- USDT / USDC – stablecoins  

Crypto is the next evolution of digital finance.
