# How to Buy and Sell Crypto

Buying crypto is simple if done safely.

## How to Buy Crypto
1. Create an account on a trusted exchange  
2. Complete KYC  
3. Add funds  
4. Buy BTC, ETH, or stablecoins  

## How to Sell Crypto
1. Go to spot trading  
2. Sell your crypto for USDT  
3. Withdraw to your bank or wallet  

## Tips
- Always double-check the network before transferring  
- Start with small amounts  
- Avoid unknown P2P buyers  

Practice makes you confident.
